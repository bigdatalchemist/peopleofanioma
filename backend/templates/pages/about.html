About People of Anioma

People of Anioma is a cultural, ethnographic and storytelling initiative designed to preserve, promote, and explore the rich heritage of the Anioma people. Through technology, data, and community-driven projects, we aim to document and celebrate the language, identity, migration patterns, oral histories, and contemporary experiences of Anioma communities both at home and in the diaspora.
